Projects in Development

1. ) A.I Controlled Headphones

These headphones are currently in development and are set to release in late 2025. They are controlled by A.I and allow for a whimsical headphones experience.

The A.I aspect of these headphones allow for an A.I to give song suggestions based on what you currently listen to alongside automatically turning up your phone at your request!

The headphones are capable of 20 hours of non-stop use and come with complete cancellation of any noise. Altogether, these headphones are able to deliver automatic use through A.I alongside a welcoming noise free experience!

2. ) Barkphones

Have you ever seen those headphones with cat ears on them? Well, have we got a treat for you! Barkphones, releasing in late 2023, these headphones feature dog ears, lifelike dog fur that is soft to the touch, an optional nosepiece and an immersive experience for dog lovers alike!

To give this immersive experience we have given the headphones a bark that comes on when the battery is low and gets louder the lower the battery gets! The bark is also sounded to signal when the headphones are turning on or powering off.

Overall, these headphones serve to immerse lovers of the man's best friend!

3. ) Heart Headphones

Do you love someone? Well, have we got a treat for you!

Releasing in 2024, these heart-shaped red velvet colored headphones can reach both you and your lover through wireless and love connection! This might sound crazy, but these headphones can sense love between two individuals and allows for both lovers to listen to their favorite music together!<

Overall, these headphones are made for you and your lover to have unforgettable days of music together!
